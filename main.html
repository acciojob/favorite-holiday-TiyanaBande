Paris, France


Paris, known as the City of Light, is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world. It's famous for its rich culture, history, and romantic ambiance. Whether you're strolling along the Seine or exploring world-class museums, Paris offers an unforgettable experience.

  1. Visit the Eiffel Tower and enjoy the stunning views.
  2. Explore the art at the Louvre Museum.
  3. Walk around the historic Notre-Dame Cathedral.
  4. Relax in the beautiful Luxembourg Gardens.
  5. Take a cruise on the Seine River at night.
  • Delicious croissants from a local bakery.
